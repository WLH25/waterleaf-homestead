Our Mission

We are a small, no-compromise family homestead in Southeast Ohio. We raise turkeys, chickens, and produce the way it should be done — slow, humane, clean, and connected to the land. No corporate fluff, just real food for real people.

As a 100% disabled veteran, I’m proud to run Waterleaf Homestead with honesty and hard work. Everything we grow or raise is handled with care, inspected when required, and delivered with integrity.
