What We Offer

Here at Waterleaf Homestead, everything we raise is done with care and integrity. Our animals are never caged. They roam freely in open pasture. They eat well. They live clean. And they are treated with the respect every living thing deserves.

We currently offer:

  • Pasture-raised chickens and turkeys
  • Farm fresh eggs and seasonal produce
  • Custom meat orders with no fillers, no chemicals, and no compromise

What you get from us is food the way it used to be. Real. Fresh. Honest. You can taste the difference, and you can see where it comes from. Our farm is not just a source of food — it is a source of trust.

What’s Coming in 2026

In 2026, we will begin offering small batch dairy. That includes:

  • Raw milk
  • Hand churned butter
  • Cream, yogurt, cheese, and more

Everything will be made right here, by hand, using clean processes and wholesome practices. No preservatives. No machines pumping out lifeless product. Just real dairy from animals we care for and respect.

And we will not stop there. Our plan is to begin hosting visitors. Kids. Families. Neighbors. Anyone who wants to experience real farm life — to learn how food is made, how animals are cared for, and how peace can be found in something as simple as a slow morning with cows and chickens.

Outreach and Community

Starting in 2026, our gates will open wider.

We are building a place where people can reconnect. A space to feel safe. Grounded. At peace.

We will be offering experiences for:

  • Children who deserve joy, adventure, and something real
  • Veterans looking for quiet, connection, and healing
  • People from all walks of life who just want to breathe again

Our programs will include day in the life farm tours, hands on animal care, and a seat at our table for those who just need it. Whether you are burned out, broken down, or simply curious, you are welcome here.

This homestead is where judgment stops and kindness begins. A place where the birds sing louder than the noise in your head and the land holds more peace than any therapist’s office ever could. We do not promise perfection, but we do promise truth.
