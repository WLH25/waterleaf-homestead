Refund Policy

Due to the cost of birds, pre-purchased feed, and ODA-inspected processing allocated per customer, no refunds will be issued for canceled orders unless the farm is at fault. This includes circumstances where the farm is unable to fulfill your order (e.g., illness in the flock, weather-related damage, etc.).
