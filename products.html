Why Our Meat is Different

Most grocery store poultry comes from birds raised in giant warehouses — never seeing sunlight, fed cheap grain, and pumped with antibiotics. Our birds live on pasture, scratch in the dirt, and grow at a natural pace. We never use cages, fillers, or shortcuts.

Our prices are a bit higher because they reflect the real cost of feed, pasture-based care, and certified ODA-inspected processing — not factory shortcuts. When you buy from us, you're feeding your family better and supporting a 100% disabled veteran from right here in Muskingum County.
